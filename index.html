Design + Strategy

Oscar Lund

My name is Oscar, and I make things. As a creative artist, I love to shape beauty. Both estethically, and functionally. Beautiful things are lovely, don't you agree? As design can be a big word, I like to refer to it as; the creation of products people want to interact with. For example, when I develop a website, I think of the development as; Creating a place where people want to spend time. Just like a living room, or a kitchen.
